Lasagne

Description

This is a lovely and tasty Lasagne It has lots of cheese and spices to give it flavor.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Mince
  • Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Kneed dough
  2. Flatten dough
  3. Add mince
  4. Spread tomato and cheese
  5. Add all ingredients
  6. Put in oven for 30 minutes
  7. Eat
