Pasta

Description

This is a lovely and tasty pasta straight from the oven. It has lots of cheese and spices to give it flavor.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Ham
  • Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Cook PAsta
  2. Sprinkle tomato and cheese
  3. Add all ingredients
  4. Cook in pan for 20 minutes
  5. Eat
